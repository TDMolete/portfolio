👋 About me

I’m an IT graduate (Advanced Diploma, 2024) based in South Africa. What drives me? Taking a concept, breaking it down into small pieces, and building something that actually works — even if it’s just a first version. I call it the MVP mindset.

During my 1-month WIL at Richfield College, I saw how communication and workflow fit together. That taught me that good code is only half the story — understanding the people who use it matters just as much.

Right now I’m exploring cybersecurity basics, cloud tools, and how data can make apps smarter. I’m also teaching myself AI agent concepts — because I believe the next MVPs will be smart out of the box.

🧱 My build approach

I don’t try to build everything at once. I figure out the core problem, sketch the simplest version that solves it, and put it in front of users. Then I learn, tweak, and expand. That’s the MVP cycle I follow — it keeps things lean and honest.
